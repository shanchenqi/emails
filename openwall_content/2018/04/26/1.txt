The details of this vulnerability were reported to the Apache UIMA Private mailing list.  This  vulnerability relates to an XML external entity expansion (XXE) capability of various XML parsers. See    https://www.owasp.org/index.php/XML_External_Entity_(XXE)_Processing for more details.  UIMA as part of its configuration and operation may read XML from various sources, which could be tainted in ways to cause inadvertent disclosure of local files or other internal content.
