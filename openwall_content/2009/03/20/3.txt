Decoding a specially crafted image file will result in unexpected process termination, Denial Of Service conditions or arbitrary code execution due to stack overflow.  LittleCMS is used by several Open Source projects including OpenJDK, Firefox and GIMP.  Affected version:  LittleCMS <=  The following packages were identified as affected as they statically include LittleCMS in their own packages.
