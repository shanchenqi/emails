It affects kernel  The patch ensured that both ptrace_attach() and the tracee are serialised by the tracee's cred_exec_mutex. If not, the race can be exploited by calling ptrace(PTRACE_ATTACH) to the task in the middle of exec(setuid_application). This could result in a local privilege escalation.
