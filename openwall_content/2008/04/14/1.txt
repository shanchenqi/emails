The issue involves libpng incorrectly handling zero length chunks which results in uninitialized memory affecting the control flow of the application.  Affected version:  The libpng project indicates through through and through built with PNG_READ_UNKNOWN_CHUNKS_SUPPORTED or PNG_READ_USER_CHUNKS_SUPPORTED (default configuration) are affected.
