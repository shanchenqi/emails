By means of a CSRF-style attack,     this can lead to the disclosure of various types of private data     stored on a wiki. All users are advised to upgrade. Use      A register_globals arbitrary inclusion vulnerability was discovered in     the beta release series, in MediaWikiParserTest.php. This     vulnerability does not affect any stable MediaWiki release. It only     affects wikis which have PHP's register_globals feature enabled,     despite our strong advice to the contrary. Apache installations with     AllowOverride enabled may be protected against this vulnerability,     since there is a .htaccess file with "Deny from all" in the relevant path.
