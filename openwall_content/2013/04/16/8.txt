Download links are given at the end of this email.  * An internal review discovered that specially crafted Lua function names could lead to XSS.  * Daniel Franke reported that during SVG parsing, MediaWiki failed to prevent XML external entity (XXE) processing. This could lead to local file disclosure, or potentially remote command execution in environments that have enabled expect:// handling.  * Internal review also discovered that Special:Import, and Extension:RSS failed to prevent XML external entity (XXE) processing.
