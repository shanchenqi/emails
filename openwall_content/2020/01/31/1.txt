It turns out a change in EOF handling introduced in sudo prevents exploitation of the bug.  The EOF character is also initialized to 0 and sudo checks for EOF before it checks for the kill character.  This means that the bug actually affects sudo versions to inclusive.  Sorry for the oversight.
