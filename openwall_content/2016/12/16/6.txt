Description: This is an information disclosure vulnerability in the short-circuit reads feature of HDFS. A local user on an HDFS DataNode may be able to craft a block token that grants unauthorized read access to random files by guessing certain fields in the token.  Mitigation: Users on 2.7.x should upgrade to or later. Users on 2.6.x or earlier releases should upgrade to or later.  Impact: A local user may be able to gain unauthorized read access to files.
