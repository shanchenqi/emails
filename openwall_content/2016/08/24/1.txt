Local users with sudo access could read (portions of) specially-formatted files with elevated privileges. Future versions of readline will make the vulnerability more significant by showing error messages for malformed entries, rather than silently ignoring them.  This flaw is distribution-specific - upstream sudo does not include INPUTRC, and we are not aware at this time of any other distros that include it.
