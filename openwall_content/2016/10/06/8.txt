Jones of Red Hat reported a vulnerability that affects OpenStack Cinder, Glance and Nova. By providing a maliciously crafted disk image an attacker can consume considerable amounts of RAM and CPU time resulting in a denial of service via resource exhaustion. Any project which makes calls to qemu-img without appropriate ulimit restrictions in place is affected by this flaw.
