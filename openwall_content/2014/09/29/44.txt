Title: Potential leak of passwords into log files Reporter: Amrith Kumar (Tesora) Products: Cinder, Nova, Trove Versions: up to versions up to  Description: Amrith Kumar from Tesora reported two vulnerabilities in the processutils.execute() and strutils.mask_password() functions available from oslo-incubator that are copied into each project's code. An attacker with read access to the services' logs may obtain passwords used as a parameter of a command that have failed or when the mask_password did not mask passwords properly.
