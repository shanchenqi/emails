Credit: This issue was discovered by Patrick Uiterwijk of Red Hat. References: Upstream patch:   Versions affected: to and Fixed in versions: Description: It was found that Ipsilon does not check whether a user is authorized to delete a service provider. This makes it possible for any authenticated user to delete any service provider, causing a denial of service.  Mitigation: Users of Ipsilon should update to version or or later. Credit: This issue was discovered by Patrick Uiterwijk and Rob Crittenden of Red Hat.
